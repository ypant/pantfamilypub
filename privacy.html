Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app.

We respect your privacy and we do not collect any user data, personal or non-personal besides your email address and name for verifying your identity when you use our app. This means we do not collect any other information such as your phone number, location, or any other data that can identify you.

We do not use any third-party services or software that might collect data from you. We also do not use cookies or any other tracking technology.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third-party for any purpose. We do not require you to create an account or sign up for any service to use our app. You can use our app anonymously to view publicly available documents. However since this app also contains private information of relationships that we have gathered through our research, you would need to register and be approved before you will be allowed to access that content.

We do not collect any information about your usage of the app. This means we do not track the pages you visit, the buttons you click, or any other information related to your use of the app.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us by visiting the contact page of the website.
